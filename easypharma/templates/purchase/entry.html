Pharmacy Keyboard Shortcuts & Workflow
Purchase Entry Workflow
  1. Press F10 to focus and select Supplier
  2. Enter Invoice Number and Date
  3. Press F9 to focus the Medicine search box
  4. Type medicine name and select it
  5. Fill in batch details (Batch No, Expiry, Qty, Cost, Sale Price)
  6. Press ↵ Enter to add the item to the list
  7. Repeat for other medicines, then press F8 to save
