POS Keyboard Shortcuts & Workflow
Add Items Workflow
  1. Press / or click search box
  2. Type medicine name (autocomplete)
  3. Press ↓ or ↑ to navigate batches
  4. Press ↵ Enter to add selected batch
  5. Enter quantity in table row
  6. Press ↵ Enter to search next item
  7. Repeat steps 2-6 for more items
