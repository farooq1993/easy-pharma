Default Payment Mode
Choose the default payment option highlighted during new sales checkout.
Require Customer Phone Number
When enabled, the POS billing system will prompt for the customer's phone number before generating invoices.
Auto Print Invoice
Open the print configuration/dialog pop-up automatically right after a sales invoice is successfully saved.
Product Expiry Date Input Format
Select how the product expiry date is entered during purchase entries. Choose between quick text input (e.g. MM/YY) or convenient Month/Year dropdown selections.
Default Tax Rate (%)
Define the default GST rate applied to new products registered during purchase invoicing.
Auto-Update Selling Price
Automatically update the product's base retail price in the master inventory when a change in purchase rate is detected.
Gmail Address
The Gmail account where you receive supplier invoices. (e.g. yourstore@gmail.com)
Gmail App Password (16-digit)
Enter the 16-character app password generated in your Google Account security settings. (Note: Normal Gmail password will not work!)
Enable Automatic Gmail Fetching
When enabled, the application background worker will search your inbox for new purchase bills periodically.
How to generate Gmail App Password?
  1. Go to your Google Account (myaccount.google.com) and navigate to the Security tab.
  2. Ensure 2-Step Verification is turned ON under "How you sign in to Google".
  3. Search for "App Passwords" in the top search bar of your Google Account dashboard.
  4. Select App as "Other (Custom name)", name it "Easy Pharma", and click Generate.
  5. Copy the 16-letter code and paste it in the field above (without spaces).
