How Indian Financial Year & Freeze Books Work
  • Invoice Number Auto-Reset: On 1st April of every new Financial Year, invoice numbers automatically restart from INV-YY-YY-0001.
  • Stock & Balance Carry Forward: Closing stock and customer/supplier ledger balances automatically carry forward into the new year.
  • Freeze Books (Lock FY): Click "Lock FY" after audit completion to seal the books. Once locked, no transactions, edits, or deletions are permitted in that year.
